Bee & Flower is a simple yet challenging game where you control a bee on its quest to reach a flower without getting crushed by raindrops. Not all raindrops have similar speeds.
The bee has a limited number of lives(3) to complete its journey, so be careful and avoid the raindrops!

- Use the directional buttons (UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT) to move the bee.
- Press ENTER to start the game.
- Press BACK to restart the game or play again after winning.

Enjoy playing Bee & Flower, and have fun guiding the bee to its destination!
